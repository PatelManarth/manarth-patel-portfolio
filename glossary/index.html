Plain-English Security

Cybersecurity Glossary

Technical terms explained for recruiters, learners, small businesses, and anyone who wants the simple version first.

Security Operations Centre

A team that monitors security activity, investigates alerts, and coordinates response.

Security Information and Event Management

A platform that collects and analyzes logs so analysts can detect and investigate suspicious activity.

Identity and Access Management

The people, processes, and technology used to control who can access which systems and data.

Multi-factor Authentication

An extra verification step that reduces the risk of stolen passwords being enough to sign in.

MITRE ATT&CK

A common knowledge base for describing adversary tactics and techniques.

Zero Trust

A security model that continuously verifies access instead of trusting users or devices by default.

Log

A record of activity generated by a system, application, user, or security tool.

Alert

A notification that activity may require investigation. An alert is not automatically an incident.

Security Incident

An event or series of events that threatens confidentiality, integrity, or availability and requires response.
